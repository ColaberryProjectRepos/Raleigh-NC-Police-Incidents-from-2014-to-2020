Raleigh, also known as City of Oaks, is the capital of the state of North Carolina in Wake County. 

As of July 1, 2019, the population is about 475,000.  While population growth across the U.S. has slowed in recent years, Raleigh's population boom continues. Car Insurance Companies shows Raleigh gained 38,196 residents between 2013 and 2018, an 8.9% increase.

North Carolina is the home to one of the safest cities in the country.  Raleigh ranks No. 9 on a list published by WalletHub with the lowest crime rates in 2020 (and lowest financial insecurity rates) in the nation.  However, homicides and non-deadly shootings in the City of Oaks were up in 2020 compared to 2019.
